From Solomon O., Founder

Chartered Accountant • Community-first

Hi, I’m Solomon O., a Chartered Accountant who has spent the last decade helping everyday people, students, immigrants, multicultural families, and small business owners untangle confusing money journeys. I’ve seen budgets written on napkins, group chats full of anxious bank screenshots, and the quiet pride when someone pays off a debt early.

FinKnow is my love letter to everyone who was told money talk isn’t for them. We mix cultural wisdom with modern AI so you never feel judged, only guided. We’ll celebrate your first £10 saved like it’s a festival, and we’ll steady you when life gets windy. You belong here, and your money story deserves to be human, hopeful, and bold.
